How to Use

  1. Highlight text on any webpage by selecting it with your mouse
  2. Click the 💡 Explain button that appears
  3. Choose your preferred depth level:
    • ⚡ Quick — 50-100 words
    • 📖 Normal — 100-200 words
    • 🔬 Detailed — 200-400 words
  4. Read the AI explanation in the overlay popup
  5. Click 🔊 Listen to hear the explanation read aloud
  6. Highlight the same concept 3+ times to auto-generate a flashcard
Tip: Press Escape to close the explanation popup.
Backend: Make sure the server is running at
